# Privacy Policy for Alles Gut

**Last Updated:** [Current Date]

## 1. Introduction
Alles Gut ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

## 2. Data Collection
We collect the minimum amount of data necessary to provide our check-in and safety services.

### 2.1 Personal Information (Encrypted)
We collect the following personal information, which is **encrypted at rest**:
-   **Display Name**: How you appear to your contacts.
-   **Contact Information**: Email address or phone number.
-   **Contacts**: The list of users you have connected with.

### 2.2 Usage Data (Anonymous)
We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our application. This data **cannot be linked back to you**.
-   **Events**: App opens, feature usage (e.g., "Export Data clicked").
-   **Session ID**: A temporary ID to track user flows within a session, not linked to your user account.

## 3. Data Storage and Security
-   **Encryption**: All sensitive personal data (names, contact info) is encrypted using AES-256-GCM before being stored in our database. We do not store your data in plain text.
-   **Location**: Data is stored in secure data centers.

## 4. How We Use Your Data
-   **Service Provision**: To enable the "Check In" feature and notify your contacts if you fail to check in.
-   **Notifications**: To send you reminders and alerts.
-   **Improvement**: Anonymous analytics are used to understand user behavior and enhance the user experience.

## 5. Your Rights
You have the following rights regarding your data:
-   **Right to Access/Portability**: You can export all your data in a machine-readable format (JSON) via the app settings.
-   **Right to Deletion**: You can delete your account at any time, which permanently erases all your data from our servers.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
